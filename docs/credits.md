## What Ahsan built

- **Product design** — feature scope, content hierarchy, bilingual model, monetisation strategy, growth loops (referrals + auto-Pro upgrade).
- **Frontend** — React 19 + Vite 8 + Tailwind v4 + Radix UI + Capacitor 8 hybrid Android app.
- **Backend** — Firebase free tier (Firestore + Auth + Hosting) — no Cloud Functions, no paid services.
- **Data pipeline** — Free scrapers for Pakistani textbook content + transforms + Firestore seeding.
- **SEO / AEO infrastructure** — Postbuild static-HTML generator producing 660+ prerendered pages with per-page JSON-LD, plus `llms.txt`, `ai.txt`, AI-bot allowlist in `robots.txt`, and build-time `sitemap.xml` + `feed.xml`.
- **Community module** — verified contributor system with submissions, voting, flagging, comments, moderator queue, achievements, strikes, bans, denylist, and audit log.
- **Observability** — Sentry, Amplitude, Microsoft Clarity, OneSignal — all wired before launch.

## How to support

If ImtehanHub helped you or your students, the most valuable things you can do — in order — are:

1. **Tell other Pakistani students.** Share the link in your class WhatsApp, your school noticeboard, or your study group. ImtehanHub is free; the only thing it needs is reach.
2. **Refer 3 friends through your referral code** — they get a free study tool, you get auto-upgraded to Pro.
3. **Contribute MCQs through the community module** — bilingual, textbook-referenced submissions are reviewed and (if approved) promoted to the official question set.
4. **Optional financial support** for the project: [aoneahsan.com/payment](https://aoneahsan.com/payment?project-id=imtehanhub&project-identifier=com.aoneahsan.imtehanhub).
