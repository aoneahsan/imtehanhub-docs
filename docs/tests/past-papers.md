# Past papers

**Past-paper practice** on ImtehanHub is a set of free landing pages that turn a board exam's pattern into solved practice. Each page is scoped to one **board × class × subject × year** — for example, the FBISE Class 10 Physics paper for 2024 — and gives you MCQ, short, and long questions modelled on that paper, referenced to your official textbook, in both English and Urdu.

> **TL;DR** — Browse from the hub at [imtehanhub.aoneahsan.com/past-papers](https://imtehanhub.aoneahsan.com/past-papers), pick your board, class, subject, and year, then practise questions built on that paper's pattern. We do **not** host copied board papers — each page offers practice built on the board's scheme of studies.

## Honest framing first

ImtehanHub does **not** republish, host, or reproduce the actual question text of any board's real past paper. Distributing a board's copyrighted paper is not something we do.

What each page gives you instead is **practice modelled on the board's pattern** — the format, the topic spread from the official scheme of studies, and the difficulty of a given year's exam — so you revise the way that board actually assesses. Every practice question is tied to a textbook chapter and page, and is available in English and Urdu.

## What's covered

Practice pages are live for these combinations:

| Dimension | Coverage |
|---|---|
| **Boards** | FBISE, BISE Lahore, BISE Karachi, BISE Rawalpindi, AKU-EB |
| **Classes** | Class 9, Class 10, 1st Year, 2nd Year |
| **Subjects** | Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Math, English, Urdu |
| **Years** | 2023 and 2024 (BISE Rawalpindi: 2024) |

That is 200 board-pattern practice pages today. Coverage grows over time — more boards, subjects, and years are added as the underlying content is built.

## How to find a past paper

Two paths:

1. **The hub** — open [`/past-papers`](https://imtehanhub.aoneahsan.com/past-papers) on the app. It groups every live page by board and class so you can click straight to yours.
2. **A direct URL** — the address of each page follows a fixed shape:

   ```
   /{board}/class-{n}/{subject}/past-papers/{year}
   ```

   For example, `/bise-lahore/class-9/chemistry/past-papers/2024` is the BISE Lahore Class 9 Chemistry practice page for 2024.

Class 11 shows as **1st Year** and Class 12 as **2nd Year** (FA / FSc / HSSC) throughout the app, matching how Pakistani students name them.

## What a past-paper page contains

Each page is a full study surface, not a stub:

- A definition-first intro explaining what that board's paper is and who sits it.
- The **topics assessed**, drawn from the board's official scheme of studies for that subject and stage.
- The **paper format** — how MCQ, short, and long sections are structured.
- A **preparation plan** and revision tips specific to the subject.
- Solved MCQ, short, and long practice questions modelled on the pattern.
- A short FAQ, and links to related boards, subjects, and the other year.

Everything renders in both English and Urdu, with right-to-left support for Urdu.

## Common questions

### Are these the actual board papers?

No. We do not host or reproduce any board's real past paper. Each page is **practice modelled on** the board's pattern and scheme of studies. That keeps the content legal and, more usefully, keeps it aligned to how the board assesses rather than to one fixed set of old questions.

### Which boards are covered?

FBISE, BISE Lahore, BISE Karachi, BISE Rawalpindi, and AKU-EB at present. More boards are added as content is built — check the [hub](https://imtehanhub.aoneahsan.com/past-papers) for the current list.

### My class isn't 9–12 — can I still use ImtehanHub?

Yes. Past-paper practice pages cover Class 9 to 2nd Year, but the wider app covers Class 5 upward. Use the [subjects browser](/docs/discovery/subjects) to find your class and chapters.

### Do I need an account?

You can read a past-paper page without signing in. To actually take a scored test, save progress, or keep a streak, [sign in](/docs/getting-started/sign-in) — it is free.

### Are the practice questions free?

Yes. ImtehanHub is free for students. See [Quotas and plans](/docs/concepts/quotas-and-plans) for the small daily-quota details on some surfaces.
